Sloppy Joes

Description

Looking for a recipe that's a little messy but totally delicious? Look no further than our world's best sloppy joes! Packed with flavor and easy to make, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who loves classic comfort food.

Our sloppy joes are made with a perfectly seasoned ground beef and onion mixture that's simmered in a rich tomato sauce, then piled high on a soft hamburger bun. The sweet and tangy flavor of the sauce is the perfect complement to the savory beef, making each bite a true taste sensation. And the best part? You can whip up a batch of these sloppy joes in just 30 minutes, making them perfect for a quick and easy weeknight dinner or a fun weekend lunch. Trust us, once you try these world's best sloppy joes, you'll be hooked!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¾ cup ketchup, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook and stir lean ground beef in the hot skillet until some of the fat starts to render, 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Add onion and bell pepper; continue to cook until vegetables have softened and beef is cooked through, 3 to 5 more minutes.
  4. Stir in ketchup, brown sugar, mustard, and garlic powder; season with salt and pepper. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 to 30 minutes.
  5. Divide meat mixture evenly among hamburger buns.
