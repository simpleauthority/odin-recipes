Lasagna

Description

Hey there, welcome to our recipes website - we're so glad you're here! We've got something really special to share with you today: our world-famous lasagna recipe that will knock your socks off.

Our lasagna is the perfect combination of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and perfectly cooked noodles, all baked to perfection under a blanket of melted mozzarella cheese. We've been making this lasagna for years, and it never fails to impress our friends and family.

Trust us, once you taste this lasagna, you'll be hooked! So grab your apron and get ready to whip up the best lasagna you've ever tasted. We promise you won't regret it.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: 1 onion and 2 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products: 1 can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce, and 2 cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: Store-bought or homemade
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg: Helps bind the ricotta

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
